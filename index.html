"When you don't have 8 limbs to play different instruments at the same time, loop it."
​
That's what I do. I am a live looping artist from Bangalore.
I started off as a singer and later realized that I would need some instrument to back me up. That's how I picked up the guitar and along with it came the versatility of the instrument to be used percussively. Don't get me wrong, I love playing with a band but there's a subtle something about live looping that pulls me back to playing as a one-man band.

Come watch me play live to see how looping works and maybe go home motivated to start a one-man band on your own.
